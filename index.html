Massive Discounts Announced on MG4 and MG ZS EV in Pakistan

By Arsalan Khattak | Published Aug 26, 2025 | 12:57 pm

MG Lyallpur has introduced a limited-time promotion on electric vehicles, offering customers savings up to Rs. 3.5 million on select models.

The dealership emphasized that this is a dealer-specific campaign and not a nationwide initiative by MG Pakistan. The offer is valid until September 10, 2025, and includes complimentary 7kW home chargers with each purchase

MG4 Excite

The MG4 Excite 2025 is now available at a reduced price of Rs. 8,545,000, down from its original price of Rs. 11,055,500. Equipped with a 168 HP electric motor and a 51.0 kWh lithium battery, the MG4 offers a range of 350 km on a single charge.

Charging time stands at approximately 7.5 hours, making it suitable for daily urban commuting. Customers who purchase the MG4 under this promotion will also receive a free 7kW home charger, enhancing convenience for at-home charging.

MG ZS EV

The dealership is also offering a significant discount on the MG ZS EV, now priced at Rs. 9,545,000, compared to its previous cost of Rs. 13,046,500. Powered by a 174 HP electric motor with a 51.1 kWh lithium battery, the ZS EV delivers a range of 350 km and can be fully charged in 5 hours.

With a top speed of 180 km/h, the ZS EV is positioned as a family-friendly SUV that combines practicality with electric performance. Buyers will also receive a free 7kW home charger with this purchase.
